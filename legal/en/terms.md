## 2. Plan Differentiation
- **Free Plan**: Data synchronization is manual. It is the user's responsibility to perform synchronization (Uplink) to back up their changes to the cloud.
- **Premium Plan**: Includes real-time automatic synchronization.

## 3. User Responsibility
It is the sole responsibility of the user to:

- Perform periodic manual synchronizations if they are a Free Plan user to avoid data loss in the event of device failure or loss.
- Manage their local reminders, which do not synchronize between devices.

- **Offline Use**: Using Saldo in offline mode carries the risk of data loss if the device is damaged, lost, or uninstalled before performing a synchronization.

## 5. Synchronization Policy
- **Operation**: In the Free Plan, the user must manually press the synchronization button. In the Premium Plan, synchronization is automatic.
- **Data Conflicts**: In case of concurrent edits, the system applies a Last-Write-Wins logic based on the update timestamp.

## 6. Backup and Recovery Policy
- **Backup**: Backup depends on cloud synchronization (Manual for Free, Automatic for Premium).
- **Recovery**: Restoration downloads clients and debts. Local preferences and reminders are NOT restored.

## 7. Data Retention Policy
- **Deleted Data**: Deletions are permanent after synchronization. There is no recovery process for data deliberately deleted by the user.
