# Privacy Policy - Saldo

Last updated: June 13, 2026.

## 1. Introduction
The Saldo application (hereinafter, "the Application") is owned by Alejandro Eduardo Fragozo Restrepo.
We are committed to protecting our users' privacy and the confidentiality of the information they manage on our platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

## 2. Information we collect
For the optimal functioning of the Application, we collect the following types of data:
*   **Registration Information**: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and optionally, your phone number and business name.
*   **Management Data (Clients and Debts)**: The Application allows users to store names, phone numbers, and balance records (debts and payments) of their own clients. This data is the user's property and is used exclusively for the internal management of their business.
*   **Device Information**: We collect device identifiers (FCM Token) necessary for sending push notifications related to balance updates.

## 3. Use of information
We use the collected information to:
*   Authenticate your access and maintain account security.
*   Synchronize your records across multiple devices through cloud services.
*   Send important notifications about transactions and account status.
*   Improve user experience and technical support.

## 4. Service providers and third parties
We use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
*   **Google Play Services**: For application management and integrated purchases.
*   **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication, real-time database, and push notifications.
*   **Google AdMob**: To show advertising in the free plan. AdMob may use advertising identifiers to personalize ads.

## 5. Data security
We value your trust in providing us with your information. Therefore, we use encryption of data in transit and Firebase security protocols to protect your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. User rights and data deletion
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
*   You can edit your profile from the "Profile" screen in the Application.
*   You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data from the Application's settings section or by contacting us via email.

## 7. Children's privacy
Our services are not directed to persons under 18. We do not allow the use of the Application by minors nor do we deliberately collect personal information from persons under 18.

## 8. Changes to this policy
We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. It is recommended to review this page from time to time for changes.
